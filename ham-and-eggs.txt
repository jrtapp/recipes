Ingredients:
 - 3 slices ham
 - 1 pat butter
 - 2 eggs
 - 1 tablespoon milk
 - 1 pinch salt
 - 1 pinch pepper
 - 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions:
  1. In a medium saucepan, brown ham in butter.
  2. Set ham aside.
  3. Whisk milk into eggs.
  4. Cook eggs in same saucepan used for ham.
  5. When eggs are just starting to firm, add salt, pepper and cayenne.
  6. Flip eggs and cook for 10 seconds.
  7. Serve eggs on top of ham.
